About Us

We are a innovation-modeled startup founded in 2020 in India, with a greater vision to harness the potential of Deep Tech, Artificial Intelligence, Robotics and Internet in India, towards building a future the millennials dream of. Self-Driving Cars, Cognitive Humanoids, Holographic Projections, Brain Cloning (or Time Travel through Worm Holes) deserve to be more than just science-fiction.

This began as off-shoot of a 20 year old's realization of what his generation was up to. Most colleges are busy producing corporate employees, instead of visionaries and enablers. The industry is imbalanced with plethora of service based companies, and little in the name of innovation and new tech. The students are in a rat race of getting jobs and internships, surviving on cramming what they need for interview and tests. The degrees have become a trade. Projects are copied from others and the internet. There are more sample projects out there, than something with 'X-factor'. Students don't think beyond the requirements of syllabus or job. Start-ups keep emerging with copied business models. And capital is being drained for short-team profit, instead of investing into a better future for the country and world alike. The country is letting go of it's talent. And then people complain ours is still not a developed country. Seems relatable, right?

Our Culture

We are a group of passionate technologists and engineers trying to go beyond the possible in an accelerated time frame. We are building the 'stuff'. We might fail at times. But we brush ourselves up and get back to the 'stuff' again with a better approach.

We believe as long as technology is reserved in the confines of research papers only, you can't act on the greater potential and create a solution that abides by fool-proof ethics while actually contributing exponentially towards advancement of human race.

We aim at bringing a product, developed end-to-end, out into an all-inclusive market pillared on affordability, unbiased cognition and an 'ethical' implementation. 

Message from CEO

"You don't sell the 'future' to the people. You have to let them experience it. Evolve your imagination beyond the possible, before evolving the 'tech'. And then bring that 'stuff' out of the labs."
